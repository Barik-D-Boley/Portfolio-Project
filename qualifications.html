Past Employment


J&R Property Maintenance Corp.

I worked at J&R Property Maintenance Corp for the majority of the 2020 summer from May to July. When I worked there, we worked under Foremen, and the Foreman that I worked with most was notorious for how hard he worked, and how long his shifts were. Upon his specific request to the owner of the company, I was his goto worker because of how hard I worked, and how rarely I complained.

Go Paddle AZ

I worked at Go Paddle AZ for the 2021 summer from May to July. This was a fun job to have, as I got to be out at Lake Pleasant all day, and I got to talk to a lot of the people that wanted to be out on the lake. This job helped get me a tan (that I lost within a month), and helped build my social skills among those that I would normally not be comfortable with.

Volunteer History


My Eagle Scout Project

In order to attain the rank of Eagle in the Boy Scouts, I had to do an Eagle Project. The project I chose to do was to put on a talent show for the Merrill Gardens Senior Living Center, in which I had to organize an hour's worth of talent. In total, there was 111 hours of volunteer hours that we helped add to the Scouting pool of volunteer hours.

Andrew's Eagle Scout Project

My older brother, Andrew, got a speeding ticket when he was driving to school, and was forced by my parents to get his Eagle. He decided to paint a house in Maryvale as that's basically the only kind of service project our family can do. When all was said and done, the house looked far better.

Cooper's Eagle Scout Project

My younger brother, Cooper, was forced to do his Eagle project when the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter day Saints rescinded their support of the BSA program. My Dad decided to not let all of his merit badges go to waste, so he decided to clean his Elementary School's chairs and desks.
